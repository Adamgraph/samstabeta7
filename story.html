S A M S T A - B E T A - 7

.. .The Story . :.



It started as an Idea on a Thursday, and on Saturday I started working on the JS Code.
Meanwhile I worked with CSS and picture creativity, also with the recording of the music.
On Sunday I created the spaceship and other optical images.
On Sunday evening I got the Idea for the name of the game "Samsta-Beta-7", which was my own mispronounce of "Some Star".
Music, Spaceship, Galaxyloop, all pictures and design are made by me.
I used on my Linux: Texteditor, Gimp, Audacity and an underrated Browser.

The Music you hear is building up and spinning around...
...because the browser is starting 2 musicloops, which have different starts (about 0.8 seconds).
Both loops are nearly Clones of each other, thats why they are perfectly rebuilding.
I recorded the music sequence with my musicinstrument.

Trouble with sound or Steering the Spaceship? Change in your Firefox Searchbar on the leftside of "https" the permission to "Allow Audio". Also possible in other Web-Browsers.
Also play in Fullscreen (F11) if you have a small Computer Monitor.
